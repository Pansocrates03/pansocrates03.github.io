Dino Blocks

Hack MTY Winner for "Best Streamlit Use"

Abstract

Dino Blocks' primary mission is to develop an engaging and captivating educational platform. Our aim is to make learning programming a fun and immersive experience for children. This platform is designed to stimulate their curiosity and interest in the world of coding from an early age.

It is our belief that by making learning fun, we can encourage children to dive deeper into the subject and cultivate a passion for programming. Our ultimate goal is to foster crucial skills in them such as logical thinking, problem-solving, and creativity. These are not only essential for coding but are also highly transferable skills that will benefit them in various aspects of their future lives.

Additionally, we hope that the early introduction of these concepts can help create a strong foundation that will make their future learning path in programming and technology easier and more natural. In the long run, Dino Blocks aims to play a significant role in preparing the next generation of innovators and problem solvers who can use technology to shape a better future.
